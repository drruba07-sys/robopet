INDOOR ROBOTIC PET

Robopet is an intelligent companion robot designed with advanced safety and navigation features. Equipped with a smart camera, it can detect intruders and alert its owner, adding an extra layer of security at home. It offers both autonomous navigation and remote-controlled movement, giving users flexibility in how they interact with it. A rotating ultrasound sensor constantly measures distances in all directions, enabling the Robopet to move smoothly and avoid obstacles.
