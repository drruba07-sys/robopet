About Us

The Robopet is an intelligent companion robot designed with advanced safety and navigation features. Equipped with a smart camera, it can detect intruders and alert its owner, adding an extra layer of security at home. It offers both autonomous navigation and remote-controlled movement, giving users flexibility in how they interact with it. A rotating ultrasound sensor constantly measures distances in all directions, enabling the Robopet to move smoothly and avoid obstacles. Additionally, built-in AR sensors monitor its surroundings to prevent falls from stairs or elevated surfaces, ensuring safe and reliable operation at all times.

Beyond security, the Robopet also serves as a helpful AI companion for students and families. It can provide interactive learning support, reminders, and guidance, making study sessions more engaging and productive. Designed with easy-to-use controls and worldwide accessibility, it ensures that anyone can operate it effortlessly. By combining safety, education, and companionship, the Robopet offers a versatile solution that enhances everyday life while delivering peace of mind and intelligent assistance.
